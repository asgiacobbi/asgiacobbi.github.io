Post-doctoral position LCQB, Sorbonne University

I am currently post-doc at Sorbonne University. I arrived in February 2022 in the Laboratory of Computational and Quantitative Biology (LCQB). Before, I was post-doc at Inria Bordeaux in the MONC team headed by Olivier Saut. In July 2019, I defended a PHD in Applied Mathematics in Amiens.
