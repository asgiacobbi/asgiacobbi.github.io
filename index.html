I am currently post-doc at Sorbonne Université. I arrivered in February 2022 in the Laboratory of Computational and Quantitative Biology (LCQB). I am working with Chloe Audebert in the group Mathematical Modeling in Biology. Previously, I was a post-doctoral fellow at Inria Bordeaux in the MONC team headed by Olivier Saut. In July 2019, I defended a PHD in Applied Mathematics in Amiens. My thesis was supervised by Youcef Mammeri and Antoine Galmiche.
