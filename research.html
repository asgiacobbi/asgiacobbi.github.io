Research topics

I am interested in the design of mathematical models with applications in Biology. This research area requires a wild range of mathematical tools such as ODEs/PDEs analysis, numerical analysis, probabilistic and/or statistical methods. By mathematical modeling, my work is to provide new knowledge about dynamics of biological processes involved in some diseases (cancer, diabete, obesity...) or to enhance the accuracy of mathematical models in order to better diagnose patients or to improve therapeutic strategies.

More precisely, my research interests include :

  • Mathematical modeling of biological systems (cell signalling pathway, tumor growth, cell population...)
  • Model parameter identification
  • Numerical simulations
  • Data-driven modeling (ML)
